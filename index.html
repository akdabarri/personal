I am an undergraduate student at Indonesia University of Education working at the intersection of artificial intelligence and education. My work focuses on designing systems that make learning programming more accessible, structured, and effective.

I am currently involved in the SYAIR Program under Prof. Haryadi Gunawi (University of Chicago), where I analyze contemporary research on AI-assisted learning and examine its applicability within Indonesian educational contexts.

My interests lie in combining technical system development with user research, particularly in scaffolding systems, adaptive interfaces, and the design of meaningful digital learning experiences.
