I am an undergraduate researcher at the Indonesia University of Education (UPI), focusing on the design and empirical evaluation of AI-mediated learning systems. My work investigates how adaptive scaffolding, interactive programming environments, and learning analytics can improve computational thinking and equitable learning outcomes.

I integrate system development with quasi-experimental research designs to ensure that technological innovation is grounded in measurable pedagogical impact.
